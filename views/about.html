What the heck is Degrees Of Kevin Bacon?

Have you ever heard of the Six Degrees Of Kevin Bacon? That is basically a game to find the link chain from any arbitrary actor to Kevin Bacon in six actors or less. This game is not that :)

Instead, this game gives you 6-8 random movies that Kevin Bacon did not perform in, along with one random movie that Kevin Bacon did perform in, and asks you to guess the Kevin Bacon movie.

And for those who don't want to play using Kevin Bacon, I also added a feature where you can enter an actor or actress of your choice to play the same game in place of Kevin Bacon.

This idea for this game was inspired by Fullstack Academy and was built using Node, Express and Sqlite. Check out my other side projects at my personal page.
